## What the data is

Every record in every table in `tracks/*/data/` is synthetic. Names, addresses, phone numbers, dates of birth, identifiers, and every other field are generated by [Faker](https://faker.readthedocs.io/) using seed=42. No real personally identifiable information from any real person appears anywhere in this repository.

The data is modeled on the *shape* of real social services systems: HIFIS 4 referral schemas, BC Coordinated Access, AIRS / 211 service taxonomy, VI-SPDAT assessment scoring, Onfleet / Routific delivery operations, and PIPA / FOIPPA / OCAP consent frameworks. The shape is real. The content is fabricated.
